Understanding Fees & Their Impact

Management Expense Ratio (MER)

  • Annual fee taken directly from the investment itself
  • Expressed as a percentage of the fund's assets
  • Example: An MER of 0.25% means you'll pay $25 annually for every $10,000 invested

Commissions

  • Fees paid to the brokerage every time you buy or sell an ETF or
  • Wealthsimple Trade offers commission-free trading
  • Traditional banks or brokerages may charge $5 to $10 per transaction

How Fees Impact Your Investments

  • Annual investment: $12,000 ($1,000 monthly)
  • Investment period: 10 years

Investment Accounts & Buying ETFs

Understanding Investment Accounts üìä

  • TFSA (Tax-Free Savings Account): Most popular choice for many Canadians. Contributions are after-tax, growth and withdrawals are tax-free.
  • RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan): Ideal for higher-income earners. Tax-deductible contributions, tax-deferred growth, withdrawals taxed as income.
  • FHSA (First Home Savings Account): Best for first-time home buyers. Combines TFSA and RRSP benefits specifically for saving towards a first home.
  • Non-Registered Account: No contribution limits, capital gains and dividends are taxable.
